About the Project

Safari is a Full-stack Laravel application for restaurants, hotels, and fast-food outlets to manage users, orders, and reservations. It is built with Laravel Livewire with Alpine.js and TailwindCSS.

Every hotel or restaurant that seeks to serve customers the right-way, recognizes that a website is an important part of the restaurant today.
