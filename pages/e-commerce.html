Digital Shop Dashboard

About this E-Commerce Project

This project is everything a business needs to sell online. The web application is business kit with Admin dashboard, customer dashboard, blog functionality, reusable components, Paypal Integration, Mpesa Integration, and many more out of the box functionality.

It is an application that any business can adopt without the hustle of building tools and features from scratch. Beyond that, the elegant admin dashboard provides the business owner with a beautiful UI to visualize the performance of their shop.

Core Benefits / Features

Process Payments Securely using platforms such as Mpesa, Paypal, and Stripe Out of the Box;

The Admin and customer dashboards are elegant tools to visualize the financial and inventory of your business;

Elegant User Interface (UI) that supports dark and light mode out of the box throughout the application;

Ability to track which products are performing in the marketplace and what your buyers are interested in using wishlist and sales tracking;

Keep Your Inventory up-to-date with integrated functionality to track all products and discover products running out of stock;

Automatic Email and Notification System to notify you and your customers on important activities via emails and text.

A thoroughly tested application to ensure everything works perfectly.
